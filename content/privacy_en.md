# Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 2026

> Pan-da does not sell personal data and is designed to work without an account.

## What data the app collects
Pan-da does not require a user account and does not ask for a child’s name, email address, location, or contact information.

## Data stored on your device
App settings, language preference, daily progress, favourites, hidden cards, and custom family-card photos are stored locally on your device. Custom photos stay in the app’s private storage and are not uploaded by Pan-da.

## iCloud sync
If iCloud is enabled for the app, selected app data may sync between your Apple devices through Apple’s iCloud infrastructure. Pan-da does not operate its own server for this data.

## Analytics and crash reports
If enabled in the app build, Pan-da may use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous, aggregated usage and crash information. This helps us understand which features are used and fix bugs. This data is not used for advertising and is not sold.

## Children’s privacy
Pan-da is intended to be used by parents, caregivers, teachers, or speech professionals together with children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Purchases and subscriptions
Any purchases or subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. Pan-da does not receive or store your payment card details.

## Third-party services
Pan-da may rely on Apple services such as App Store purchases and optional iCloud sync. If analytics or crash reporting is enabled, Google Firebase may be used for aggregated analytics and crash diagnostics.

## Deleting data
You can delete locally stored app data by deleting the app from your device. Data synced through iCloud may also need to be removed from your iCloud settings.

## Changes to this policy
We may update this policy when the app changes. The latest version will be published on this page.

## Contact
Questions about privacy? Contact us through the App Store listing or Instagram: [@pan_da_app](https://www.instagram.com/pan_da_app).
