# Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 2026

> PAN_DA does not sell personal data and is designed to work without an account.

## What data the app collects
PAN_DA does not require a user account and does not ask for a child’s name, email address, location, or contact information.

## Data stored on your device
App settings, language preference, daily progress, favourites, hidden cards, and custom family-card photos are stored locally on your device. Custom photos stay in the app’s private storage and are not uploaded by PAN_DA.

## iCloud sync
If iCloud is enabled for the app, selected app data may sync between your Apple devices through Apple’s iCloud infrastructure. PAN_DA does not operate its own server for this data.

## Children’s privacy
PAN_DA is intended to be used by parents, caregivers, teachers, or speech professionals together with children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Purchases and subscriptions
Any purchases or subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. PAN_DA does not receive or store your payment card details.

## Deleting data
You can delete locally stored app data by deleting the app from your device. Data synced through iCloud may also need to be removed from your iCloud settings.

## Changes to this policy
We may update this policy when the app changes. The latest version will be published on this page.
