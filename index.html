Who is TalkCards for?

👶

Children with speech delay

Designed for developmental dysphasia (vývojová dysfázie), ages 2–7. Cards are organized by communication levels — from first words to full sentences.

👨‍👩‍👦

Parents who practice at home

The app recommends what to practice today based on your child's history. No guessing. 5–10 minutes a day, guided by what therapists actually recommend.

🌍

Bilingual families

Practice the same flashcards in Czech, English, German, Portuguese, Spanish or French. Switch languages mid-session — the cards adapt instantly.

How it works

1

Today's recommendation

Every day the app suggests which category to practice — based on what your child hasn't seen recently and what matches their communication level.

2

Practice together

Pick a training mode and go through the flashcards together. Repeat words, find images by name, listen to animal sounds, complete phrases. The app speaks every word aloud.

3

Report for your therapist

TalkCards tracks accuracy and session history. Export a PDF report and bring it to your next speech therapy appointment.

5 training modes

Each mode builds a different skill. Repeat is always free.

Built for daily use

✈️

Works offline

All cards and audio work without internet. Practice in the car, on a plane, or anywhere.

🔒

Private by design

No account required. No ads. No tracking. Your child's data never leaves your device — everything syncs privately via iCloud.

📅

Daily recommendations

The app learns what you've practiced and suggests the right category each day — so you never have to decide what to work on.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family & classmates cards

Add photos of family members and classmates. TalkCards teaches your child the names of the people in their life.

📊

Therapist reports

Export a PDF with session history, accuracy by category, and progress over time. Bring it to your next appointment.

🎚️

Communication levels

Set your child's current stage — from nonverbal to full sentences. The app adapts which cards and modes are shown.

Made for Matyáš

TalkCards started as a home project when our son Matyáš was diagnosed with developmental dysphasia. We wanted something we could actually use at home — between therapy sessions, in the car, before bed. Something that felt like play, not homework. We couldn't find it, so we built it.

If you're a parent going through the same thing — this was made for you.

— Martin & Hannah, Prague
