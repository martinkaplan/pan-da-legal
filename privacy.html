Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Pan-da does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app works fully offline and requires no account.

What data we collect

We collect no personal data. Specifically:

  • No name, email address, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No health or medical information
  • No payment information (purchases are handled entirely by Apple)

How the app stores data on your device

All app data — your settings, language preference, daily progress, favourites, and hidden cards — is stored locally on your device.

If you have iCloud enabled, this data syncs to your other Apple devices via Apple's iCloud infrastructure. It is never sent to our servers (we don't have any).

Family card photos you add are stored in the app's private folder on your device. They are never uploaded anywhere.

Anonymous analytics

Pan-da uses Firebase Analytics (by Google) to collect anonymous, aggregated usage data. This includes:

  • Which training modes are used (e.g. Repeat, Find, Sounds)
  • App language setting
  • General app usage patterns

This data:

  • Is not linked to your identity in any way
  • Cannot be used to identify individual users
  • Is never sold or shared with advertisers
  • Is used only to understand how the app is used and improve it

You can review Google's privacy practices at policies.google.com/privacy.

Children's privacy

Pan-da is designed for use by parents and caregivers with young children. The child does not interact with the app independently in a way that could generate personal data. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment information. Manage subscriptions in your Apple ID settings.

Third-party services

  • Apple iCloud — optional sync of settings and progress across your devices
  • Firebase Analytics (Google) — anonymous usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google) — anonymous crash reports to help fix bugs

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
